Rupee Collector

Here's how you play the game
  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game. "Number To Reach"
  2. There are four rupees to the right.
  3. By clicking on a rupee, you will add a certain amount of points to your total score.
  4. To win the game, you need to match your total score to the Number To Reach.
  5. You will lose the game if your total score goes above the Number To Reach.
  6. The value of each rupee is unknown to you until you click on it.
  7. Each time when the game starts, the value of each rupee will change.
